First5

First5 is a mobile app and website that offers daily devotions and biblical content to help individuals start their day with focus on God. The app and website is provided by Proverbs 31 Ministries, a Christian non-profit organization that aims to inspire and encourage women in their faith journey. The devotions are short, typically 5 minutes or less, making it easy for individuals to fit into their daily routine. Each devotion includes a Bible verse, reflection, and prayer, to help individuals connect with God and set their intentions for the day. Additionally, First5 offers a range of resources including a journal, study guides and a community forum for users to share their experiences and support one another in their spiritual growth. The goal of First5 is to help individuals start their day with a deeper connection to God and a renewed sense of purpose.

  • My direct contributions:
    • Created a favorites page which stores all of the user’s favorite bible passages all in one place
    • Incorporated a custom media player allowing the user to play audio and podcasts throughout the app
    • Improved, adjusted, and fixed styling throughout the entire app
    • Responsible for release new builds to staging and production
